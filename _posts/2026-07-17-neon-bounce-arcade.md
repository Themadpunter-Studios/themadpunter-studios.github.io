Neon Bounce Arcade is the upcoming hit sequel to the game Neon Bounce. Never heard of it? Most people didn't, I mostly made it for
myself. But I released the original game on Newgrounds a little while back to semi positive reception... oh well. Now, anyways,
Neon Bounce Arcade is vastly improving on the original game. The game progresses better, has a waves system (as well as the classic mode),
more and better megastreaks, some cool gamemodes, and a lot more. I've really been enjoying playing it.

Well, when I say "upcoming", I really just mean unfinished and unreleased. You can play it right now, technically, at
[https://neonbouncearcade.themadpunter.tech](https://neonbouncearcade.themadpunter.tech), and install it as a PWA. It'll be officially released on various platforms 
(hopefully the Play Store this time) when it's ready to be released. And if you find any bugs, you should email me or something.
There's a contact page if you want to do so.

Some of my goals for Neon Bounce Arcade include:
* Web, APK, Newgrounds, itch.io release
* Some updates with new content based on how people enjoy the original game
* Possibly a Play Store release (probably will be coming in late October, and published with family comics studios)
* Maybe even a console release? It has controller support, you know...

I know, maybe I'm aiming too high. But I'm really enjoying playing the game. Even in playtesting I really am enjoying it.
And I hope that other people will enjoy it too.
